All About Ben

Ben Souvignier is currently a System's Administrator and Video Producer for ECM Publishers and a student at Hennepin Technical College taking a variety of computer courses. He is continually furthering his education to remain competitive in the ever changing world of technology.

In his free time, Ben enjoys to traveling to both exotic locales and urban utopias, participating in fun runs, such as Cupid's Undie Run and City Adventure Races, and doing something adventurous, such as the Great Bull Run, Skydiving, Rock Climbing, or White Water Rafting. Ben is also an avid fan of craft beers and liquors and touring their breweries and distilleries.
